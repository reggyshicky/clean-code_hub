Understanding INFJs

INFJs are known for their deep insights, empathy, and understanding of others. They are often seen as sensitive and compassionate individuals who are good listeners and seek to help others. They are also known for their creative and visionary thinking, often pursuing meaningful and purposeful endeavors.

What constitutes INFJS

In a broader sense, an intuitive person tends to have a strong gut feeling or instinctive understanding of things, enabling them to make quick and accurate judgments in various situations. Intuition often complements conscious reasoning and can be valuable in decision-making processes, particularly in situations where time is limited or complex information needs to be processed rapidly.
